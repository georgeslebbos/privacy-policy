Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your data from our app, follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject "Delete My Facebook Data".
  2. Include your Facebook account ID or name you used to log in.
  3. We will delete your data (if stored) within 7 days and confirm by email.

Note: We do not store any user data on our servers. Facebook Login is used only for authentication.
